## Current Top Priorities

### 1. Pipeline & Demand Generation
Driving top-of-funnel growth and qualified pipeline is the primary commercial focus. This likely means:
- Building or optimising channels that generate predictable, measurable lead flow.
- Aligning marketing activity with sales targets and revenue goals.
- Reporting pipeline contribution clearly to the CEO and board.

When helping Bron with demand gen work, prioritise specificity over theory. Channel recommendations, messaging angles, and campaign ideas should be grounded in what works for SMB B2B SaaS buyers.

### 2. Brand & Positioning
Sharpening the brand story and market differentiation. At Series A-B, positioning is often still being refined. This means:
- Helping the company say clearly what it does and why it matters.
- Differentiating from crowded categories.
- Making sure the brand voice is consistent across all touchpoints.

When helping with positioning work, push back on vague or generic claims. Good positioning is specific, defensible, and written for a real human, not a committee.

### 3. Content & Thought Leadership
Building credibility and audience through content. This includes:
- Bron's own LinkedIn presence and thought leadership.
- Company content strategy and editorial direction.
- Supporting sales with content assets that move deals forward.

Content should sound like a smart person talking, not a marketing team's third draft. See voice-guide.md for specifics.
