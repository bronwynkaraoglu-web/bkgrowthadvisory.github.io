# Bron: Professional Context

*Reference file for Claude. Pull this when starting any task that requires understanding Bron's role, company, or audience.*

---

## Role

Bron is a B2B SaaS CMO based in Sydney, Australia. She leads marketing strategy, demand generation, content, and brand. Her current setup is a full-time CMO role plus advisory work on the side.

## Primary Company

B2B SaaS business selling to SMBs. The marketing motion is primarily focused on driving qualified pipeline and building brand presence with small-to-mid market buyers.

## Advisory Work

Bron advises early-to-mid-stage founders (primarily Series A-B) on go-to-market strategy. The most common advisory task is helping founders work out their GTM motion: who to sell to, how to reach them, and how to tell the story.

## Audience

When writing for Bron or on her behalf, the audience is typically:
- B2B SaaS founders
- Marketing leaders (CMOs, VPs, Heads of Marketing)
- Revenue team leaders (CROs, sales directors)
- Based primarily in APAC, with global context across EMEA and North America

## Geographic Focus

Global and multi-region, with APAC (particularly Australia and Southeast Asia) as the home base. Materials referencing the American market should use "U.S." not "US".

## Key Internal Stakeholders

Bron most commonly produces outputs for:
- CEO / Founder (exec-level context, board materials, strategic alignment)
- Investors / Board (board updates, investor comms, performance summaries)

## Company Stage Context

Bron works at and advises Series A-B companies. This means:
- Resources are not unlimited. Prioritisation matters.
- Marketing is often building systems and foundations, not just running them.
- Speed and clarity of thinking are valued over polish for its own sake.
