About

I have been in the room when it matters most

CMO. 5x ARR growth ($15M → $100M). Two exits. Global GTM across APAC, EMEA, and North America.

I've spent 15+ years building and leading marketing functions inside B2B SaaS companies where the mandate was grow, and grow efficiently. The work was to find the right market, clarity of message, build the operating model, and turn marketing into a revenue function.

As CMO at Ignition, I built and led the global marketing function as the company scaled from $15M to $100M ARR. That included rebuilding GTM across regions and segments, leading the rebrand from Practice Ignition to Ignition, creating the revenue engagement category narrative, and turning the US into the primary growth market.

Before that, I was part of the marketing leadership team at SiteMinder, helping scale the business from employee #87 to $100M+ ARR across 27+ markets on the path to its $1B ASX listing. At Genbook, I led marketing and sales through to acquisition by Booksy, building the acquisition engine across Australia and the US.

"I build with the end in mind. That changes what you prioritise, how you scale, and what you measure."

I work best with founders and executive teams who need marketing to become a clearer, stronger, more accountable growth function.
